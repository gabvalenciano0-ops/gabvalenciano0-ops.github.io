Who We Are

We are dedicated to providing clean, safe, and high-quality drinking water to our customers.

5S Water Refilling Station is a trusted local provider of clean, safe, and purified drinking water located in Salud Mitra, Baguio City. We proudly serve households, students, and small establishments that depend on our reliable refilling and delivery services.

Our station follows strict sanitation and purification standards to guarantee high-quality and safe drinking water for the community. At 5S, every drop is filtered with care—ensuring freshness, purity, and protection for every family we serve.
