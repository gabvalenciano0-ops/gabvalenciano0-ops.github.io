Our Story

5S Water Refilling Station was established 13 years ago through a partnership between two relatives. After some time, one partner withdrew due to personal reasons, leaving full ownership to the uncle of the current manager.

With dedication, proper management, and a strong commitment to quality, the business gradually grew and earned the trust and loyalty of its customers.

Today, the station continues to provide accessible, safe, and affordable purified water to the community. With a vision for expansion and an unwavering commitment to excellent customer service, 5S aims to reach more households and businesses while maintaining the purity and reliability it is known for.
